# AI Automation & Data Analytics Bootcamp — Master Roadmap

> **Your Goal:** Become employable as an AI-powered Data Analyst & AI Automation Engineer.  
> **Your Identity:** Someone who builds AI-powered business automation systems using Python, SQL, AI, APIs, Power BI, and UiPath.  
> **Estimated Duration:** 8–9 months to complete all phases. Job-ready skills emerge after Phase 4 (~5–6 months).

### Phase 6 (Week 31–34)
